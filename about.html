Professional Summary

Crafting Sound for
Screen & Stage

I am a passionate Audio Engineer specializing in sound design, audio editing, mixing, and mastering for film, music, and digital media. My work focuses on delivering immersive audio experiences that enhance storytelling and emotional depth. I work with professional digital audio workstations and advanced plugins to achieve high-quality sound production.

Based in Nagercoil, Tamil Nadu, I have worked on a wide range of projects including dubbing for regional cinema, independent music releases, short film audio post, and live sound — building a reputation for quality, reliability, and creative sensitivity.

Every project I work on begins with a deep understanding of the story being told. Whether it's finding the perfect SFX layer for a dramatic scene, engineering clean dialogue in a dubbing session, or crafting a mix that makes music feel larger than life — the goal is always the same.

🏆 First Prize — VIT University Short Film Competition

Our team won the First Prize at the VIT University Short Film Competition. I handled the complete audio pipeline — sound design, SFX, dialogue mix, music sync, and final mastered deliverable submitted under competition specifications. This recognition reflects the power of professionally crafted audio in storytelling.

"serve the emotion, serve the narrative — great audio is invisible, and the audience should feel transported, not aware of the craft."
